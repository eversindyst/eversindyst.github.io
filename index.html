What is my quest? The goal of Argtura is to defeat the High King, Zurguran. The game will play itself for the most part, handling movement, combat, and looting. You will have to manage your character to ensure you are able to complete your quest. You have the ability to select which zone your character explores, what gear your character wears, and what skills and spells they have at their arsenal.
What are my stats? Your character has the following Stats:
Level
Your Level is an indicator of how strong you are. When exploring a new zone be sure to use your level as a reference for the zone's difficulty.

Strength
Your Strength is a measurement of how powerful you are. Every 2 Strength increases your Base Damage by 1 and every 5 Strength increases your HP by 1.

Intelligence
Your Intelligence indicates your mental capacity. Every 2 Intelligence increases your Spell Damage by 1% and every 5 Intelligence increases your Mana by 2.

Dexterity
Dexterity is how swift and accurate you are. Every 2 Dexterity increases your Stamina by 1 and every 5 Dexterity increases your Critical Strike Chance by 1 and your Critical Strike Damage by 5.

Endurance
Your Endurance is a measurement of how much your body can endure. Every 5 Endurance increases your Stamina and HP by 1 and every 10 Endurance increases your Armor by 1%.

Wisdom
Wisdom is a measurement of problem solving and reasoning skills. Every 5 Wisdom increases your Mana by 2 and every 10 Wisdom increases your Resist by 1%.

Agility
Your Agility is a measurement of how quick and fleet you are. Every 2 Agility increases your Stamina and Mana by 1 and every 10 Agility increases your Evasion by 1%.

Armor
Armor reduces the damage you take from physical attacks. Physical attacks are color coded white and is the most common attack type.
